Concept Art Pipelines When AI Joins the Party

AI isn’t replacing illustrators; it’s speeding up ideation. Here’s the guard-rail-heavy pipeline we rely on.

Sep 15, 2025 6 min read

Prompt Boards with Guard Rails

Art leads create a Notion card for every environment with the emotion, lens, forbidden motifs, and lore snippets. Midjourney exploration happens only within those rails, and every image is tagged with prompt plus seed for traceability.

Paint-Over Sprints Keep the Human Voice

Illustrators pick the most promising explorations and paint over them to inject narrative props, correct proportions, and add story lighting. The base AI image stays on a hidden layer so the PSD shows exactly how much human polish was added.

Perforce Discipline

The Midjourney JPG, layered PSD, and client-ready PNG live inside a single changelist. Producers reviewing a drop can literally scrub the pipeline and understand who touched what.

  • Consistent naming for AI explorations.
  • Automated diff exports for quick reviews.
  • Versioned brushes and LUTs alongside art files.

Client Transparency Builds Trust

We present before/after boards so stakeholders see AI as an accelerator, not a replacement. Showing the delta keeps budgets intact and reassures legal teams that IP control is maintained.
