About the Author

Rahilsha Diwan is a multifaceted creator and storyteller with a passion for blending technology and imagination. With over two years of experience in the IT and creative industries, Rahilsha has authored multiple books, including "Shattered Realities", "Secrets of Happiness" and "Me, Myself and Lucy".

As a web designer, graphic artist, and game developer, Rahilsha brings a unique perspective to his stories — combining real-world insights with fantastical elements that challenge the mind and inspire the soul. When he's not writing, he's busy building his creative studio, Cinetoon Studios, and helping other brands shine.
