About the Author

Rahilsha Diwan is a multifaceted creator and storyteller with a passion for blending technology and imagination. Based in Anand, Gujarat, India, Rahilsha has over two years of experience in the IT and creative industries, and has authored multiple books, including "Shattered Realities", "Secrets of Happiness", and "Me, Myself and Lucy".

As a web designer, graphic artist, and game developer, Rahilsha brings a unique perspective to his stories — combining real-world insights with fantastical elements that challenge the mind and inspire the soul. His background in game development and digital design influences his narrative style, creating immersive worlds that readers can visualize and experience.

When he's not writing, Rahilsha is busy building his creative studio, Cinetoon Studios, where he helps businesses establish their digital presence through game development, web design, and branding. His work spans across various creative domains, from developing mobile games to designing professional websites and creating memorable brand identities.

Rahilsha's books are available on Amazon, where readers can explore his unique storytelling style and discover stories that blend imagination with real-world wisdom. Whether you're a fan of fantasy fiction, contemporary narratives, or thought-provoking self-help content, his books offer something for every reader.
