Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some common questions about our services and process.

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline depends on the project complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects may take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! We offer maintenance and support packages to keep your website or application updated, secure, and running smoothly. We're always here to help with updates, bug fixes, and new features.

Can you help with SEO optimization?

Absolutely! SEO is built into every website we create. We optimize for search engines, improve page speed, implement proper meta tags, and follow best practices to help your site rank higher in search results.

What platforms do you develop games for?

We develop games for Android, iOS, and PC platforms. Our expertise includes mobile games (hyper-casual, puzzle, action) and PC games using Unity and Unreal Engine.

How do you handle project communication?

We maintain regular communication through email, WhatsApp, and scheduled video calls. You'll receive regular updates on project progress and can reach out anytime with questions or feedback.

Do you work with clients outside of Gujarat?

Yes! While we're based in Anand, Gujarat, we work with clients across India and internationally. Remote collaboration is seamless with our communication tools and project management processes.
